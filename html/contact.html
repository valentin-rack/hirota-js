HIROTA is a Japanese manufacturing house specializing in premium karate-gi and karate equipment. Founded in 1965, the company has established a long-standing reputation for precision craftsmanship, material excellence, and uncompromising quality. HIROTA uniforms are trusted and worn worldwide by masters, instructors, and elite competitors.
